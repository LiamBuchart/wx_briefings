# Climate Products

Long term climate products. Many are products for download rather than viewers

==========

### Canadian Surface Reanalysis

> > [CaSR](https://hpfx.collab.science.gc.ca/~scar700/rcas-casr/)

> Downscaled ERA5 data at 10km resolution. Easy to download, going back to 1980. 

-----------

### ERA5 Viewer

> > [ERA5](https://era-explorer.climate.copernicus.eu/?lat=53.44&lng=-113.47&plot=0)

> Simple viewer of ERA5 data. This page includes many graphs in climate values from any point on the globe.
