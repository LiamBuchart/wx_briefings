# Forecast Models

A variety of model forecast websites. Note that only a few of these websites focus on Canada, it is often a necessary compromise to use a broad domain to forecast for Canada. 

### Windy
>> [Windy](https://www.windy.com/-Menu/menu?500h,54.835,-103.298,3,i:gh,m:eZyaeym)

> One of the more unique forecast pages. A variety of weather variables that can be adjusted for height. Users can also add plugins, the most useful of which is the is the MeteoBlue plugin. These are some of the best meteograms available.

### Tropical Tidbits
>> [Forecast Models](https://www.tropicaltidbits.com/analysis/models/)

> Southern US focued weather page. Focused information on the Atlantic Basin hurricanes. Really good satellite imagery for the tropics. Additionally, a wide range of models including deterministic and probabilisitic models. Additionally can look at short term seasonal forecast on a national scale. >> [FireSmoke](https://firesmoke.ca/)
